FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

How long does a typical website project take?

A standard 5-page website takes approximately 3-4 weeks from initial consultation to launch. Complex projects or e-commerce websites may take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our strategy session.

What's included in your monthly packages?

Our monthly packages include website design/development, branding, social media management, content creation, SEO, and ongoing support. Each package is tailored to business size and needs. See our Services page for full details.

What's the process after I submit the contact form?

1. We'll contact you within 24 hours to schedule a free consultation.
2. We'll have a strategy session to understand your needs.
3. We'll prepare a custom proposal and quote.
4. Once approved, we'll start your project with a detailed timeline.
